Title: PyTorch: A Comprehensive Overview

"Today, I'll be discussing PyTorch, a powerful deep learning framework that has gained significant traction in both academia and industry. PyTorch was initially released by Facebook's AI Research lab in October 2016, with key contributions from Soumith Chintala, Adam Paszke, and Gregory Chanan."

Organization Background

"PyTorch was founded in 2016 under Facebook's AI Research lab. The mission behind PyTorch is to provide a flexible and efficient platform for deep learning research and application development. The vision is to democratize AI, making it accessible to researchers and developers across various domains."
